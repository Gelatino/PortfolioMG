Mail-Bag

2020 - Director, Editor

Synopsis

A woman lives vicariously through the letters she needs to deliver until she finds one whose destiny she does not want to accept.

Director's Note

The project was developed in Berlin during the Covid-19 pandemic when the entire country was in strict lockdown. While many worked from home during this period, it is not an option for the antagonist's profession.

Although not explicitly, the script tries to explore the dilemma faced by this group of workers, who kept knocking on empty doors.
